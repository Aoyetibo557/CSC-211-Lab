ABOUT US

'WOA', WAS FIRST ESTABLISHED IN THE YEAR 2000, BY JAMES ELIGNTON WITH HIS LONG TIME FRIEND KIND JOE, RIGHT AFTER THEY BOTH GRADUATED FROM THE COLLEGE OF HOGWARTS, WITH B.A IN LITERRATURE AND ART, IN THE CITY KNOWN FOR ITS MAGIC. THIER MISSION WAS TO FIND A WAY TO EXPOSE ART TO EVERYONE AND EXPRESS THE BEAUTY AND THE IMPACT IT HAS ON SOCIETY.

OUR COLLECTION CONSISTS OF OVER 1200 LOCAL AND INTERNATIONAL ARTIST. RANGING FROM CONTEMPORARY ART TO STREET ART. WE OCCASSIONALY HOST ART SHOWS AND AUCTIONS TO PROMOTE UPCOMING ARTIST AT ANY OF OUR STUDIOS AROUND THE WORLD. WORKS OF ART CURRENTLY HAS OVER 1500 EMPLOYEES AND NETWORK IN EUROPE, ASIA, SOUTH AMERICA AND NORTH AMERICA AND ITS LOOKING TO EXPAND ITS REACH INTO OTHER CONTINENTS.
